GET TO KNOW ME

About Me

Creativity, Science, and Visual Storytelling.

I am Jake Christ Ruiz Mejares, a 3rd Year Bachelor of Secondary Education major in Science student at the University of Science and Technology of Southern Philippines – Cagayan de Oro.

Beyond my journey in science education, I express myself through creativity and visual communication. I am a graphic designer and digital artist who enjoys transforming ideas into meaningful and engaging visual experiences.

I am also involved in journalism as an editorial cartoonist, where I use illustration and visual storytelling to communicate ideas, perspectives, and social issues. Through my creative work, I aim to create designs and artworks that are not only visually appealing but also purposeful and impactful.

My experiences in creative work, journalism, and student life continue to develop my skills in communication, critical thinking, creativity, collaboration, and leadership.
